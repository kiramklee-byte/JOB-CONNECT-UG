About Job Connect Uganda

The Problem

Many job seekers in Uganda facesystemic barriers including information gaps, skills mismatch, lack of career guidance, and geographic isolation. As a result, youth unemployment remains a critical challenge.

Our Mission

To reduce unemployment by providing a transparent, accessible, and skill-focused platform that connects talent with opportunity.

Our Vision

A Uganda where every young person has access to meaningful employment and career growth opportunities.

Objectives

  • Provide up-to-date job listings
  • Connect job seekers with employers
  • Enable users to create and manage professional profiles
  • Provide career guidance and development content
  • Promote internships and training opportunities
  • Reduce unemployment levels among the youth

Target Users

  • Unemployed youth and recent graduates
  • Employers (SMEs and large corporations)
  • Recruitment agencies
  • Vocational and professional training institutes
  • Career mentors and consultants
