Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Job Connect Uganda free for job seekers?

Yes! Creating a profile, uploading your CV, and applying for jobs is completely free.

2. How much does it cost to post a job?

Basic job postings are free. Premium listings start at 50,000 UGX for featured placement.

3. How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

4. Can I edit my CV after uploading?

Yes, log into your dashboard and upload an updated version anytime.

5. How do employers find my profile?

Employers can search candidate profiles based on skills and location. Keep your profile complete!

6. Do you offer internships?

Yes, many employers post internship opportunities. Check the job listings regularly.
